Prepare to die edition

Dark Souls: Prepare to Die Edition is the PC release, as well as the PS3 and Xbox 360 re-release, of Dark Souls. The game was released on August 24th, 2012 for PC and in October 2012 for PS3 and Xbox 360 and includes the Artorias of the Abyss expansion which introduced new bosses, weapons and locations. The Artorias of the Abyss DLC is also available as a separate download for Xbox 360 and PS3 owners.

Minimum system requirements

  • OS: Windows XP, Windows Vista, Windows 7 or newer
  • CPU: Intel Core 2 Duo E6850 GHz+ or AMD Phenom II X2 545 3.0 GHz+
  • RAM: 2 GB
  • HDD: 4 GB
  • VGA: GeForce 9800 GTX+ or ATI Radeon HD 4870+
  • DX: 9.0c
  • Sound: Direct Sound Compatible

Dark Souls Remastered

In a surprise Nintendo Direct conference on January 11, 2018, it was announced that Dark Souls would be remastered and ported to the Nintendo Switch, Xbox One, PlayStation 4, and PC. The remaster released on PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC on May 24 and 25, 2018. The Nintendo Switch release was originally planned for May 25 as well, but was delayed to summer 2018 On August 14, 2018, it was announced that the Switch version would be releasing on October 19, 2018. The remaster includes the Artorias of the Abyss expansion and improved graphics, lightning, shading, and sound effects and runs at 60 frames per second on both consoles and PC. The multiplayer component received dedicated servers for all four platforms, with up to 6 players. The game will cost $39.99 US, but offers a 50% discount for owners of the original Dark Souls on PC.

  • OS: Windows 7 64-bit, Service Pack 1
